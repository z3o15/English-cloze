大作文写作指南

基本要求
  • 格式：不写标题
  • 字数：字数适当，180词左右
  • 字迹：字迹工整，尽量不涂改（写错单词轻轻划掉即可）
三段式结构 (7/8句)
    开头段 (2/3句)
  • 引出图表
  • 描述图表
  • 比大小/比趋势
    • 主体段 (2/3句)
  • 分析原因
  • 有时间变化 重点「比趋势」
  • 无时间变化重点「比大小」
    • 总结
  • 评价+建议/预测
"比大小"类图表写作指南
引出图表句型

that+句子： 调查对象 have different purposes of / show different attitudes towards 图表话题

Based on the data offered, one can clearly see that 描述对象1 ranks the first/highest among all the categories, accounting for 数据1. Next are 描述对象2 and 描述对象3 (with 数据2 and 数据3 respectively), while 描述对象4 only constitutes 数据4.

引出话题句型
From my perspective, it is of no difficulty to come up with two key factors to account for the scene.
从我的角度来看想出解释这一现象的两个关键因素并不困难。
2个原因句型
The first contributing factor is that +句子。
第一个起作用的因素是
Another important factor that cannot be ignored is that +句子
另一个不能忽视的重要因素是。
评价句型
积极
In view of the analysis above, we can conclude that it is of little surprise to see this scene in the current era.
鉴于以上分析，在当今时代看到这种现象并不奇怪。
消极
In view of the analysis above, we can conclude that it is surprising to see this phenomenon in the current era.
鉴于以上分析，在当今时代看到这种现象是令人震惊的。
预测/建议句型
积极-预测
Therefore, it can be predicted that 描述对象 will still take up a large share in the future.
因此，可以预测描述对象在未来也将占据很大份额。
消极-建议
Therefore, we need to take actions to address the problem.
因此，我们需要采取行动来解决这一问题。

完整范文示例
2016饼图范文（静态图通用）
[饼图：大学生旅游目的分布]

The chart clearly illustrates that college students have different purposes of traveling. Based on the data offered, one can clearly see that enjoying the beautiful scenery ranks the first among all the categories, accounting for 37%. Next are relieving stress, cultivating independent abilities and others with 33%, 9% and 15% respectively, while making friends only constitutes 6%.

From my perspective, it is of no difficulty to come up with two key factors to account for the scene. To begin with, the first contributing factor is that traveling around enables college students to interact with local people and develop interpersonal relationship with them. They can also experience local culture in-depth. In addition, another significant factor that cannot be ignored is that they can make friends with people from different culture backgrounds and enjoy the good view so that they can reduce their pressure.

In view of the analysis above, we can conclude that it is of little surprise to see this scene in current era. (An increasing number of college students are traveling in their spare time. ) Therefore, it can be predicted that reducing stress will take up a large share in the future.

该图表清晰显示大学生有不同的旅游目的。根据提供的数据，我们可以清楚地看出欣赏美景在所有类别中排名第一，占37%。其次是缓解压力、培养独立能力和其他，分别占33%、9%和15%，而交朋友只占6%。

从我的角度来看，想出解释这一现象的两个关键因素并不困难。首先，第一个起作用的因素是环游使大学生能够与当地人互动并发展人际关系。他们还可以深入体验当地文化。此外，另一个不容忽视的重要因素是他们可以与来自不同文化背景的人交朋友并欣赏美景，从而减轻压力。

鉴于以上分析，我们可以得出结论，在当今时代看到这种现象并不奇怪。（越来越多的大学生正在业余时间旅行。）因此，可以预测减轻压力在未来将占据很大份额。

2017折线图范文（动态图通用）
[折线图：中国博物馆数量及访客数量变化]

The chart clearly illustrates the remarkable changes in the number of museums and their visitors in China during the past several years. Based on the data offered, one can clearly see that the number of museums rose significantly from 4165 in 2013 to 4697 in 2015, while that of their visitors experienced a dramatic increase during the same period, reaching 781.1 million in 2015.

From my perspective, it is of no difficulty to come up with two key factors to account for the scene. To begin with, the first contributing factor is that visiting museums enables people to appreciate numerous exhibits from around the world and enjoy more diverse culture. In addition, another significant factor that cannot be ignored is that visitors can obtain fresh visiting experience so that they can unwind and recharge themselves quickly.

In view of the analysis above, we can conclude that it is of little surprise to see this scene in the current era. Therefore, it can be predicted that the number of museums and their visitors in China will still keep this trend of growth in the future.

该图表清晰显示了中国博物馆数量及其访客数量在过去几年里的显著变化。根据提供的数据，我们可以清楚地看出博物馆数量从2013年的4165家显著增加到2015年的4697家，而其访客数量在同一时期经历了急剧增长，在2015年达到7.811亿。

从我的角度来看，想出解释这一现象的两个关键因素并不困难。首先，第一个起作用的因素是参观博物馆使人们能够欣赏世界各地的众多展览并享受更多元的文化。此外，另一个不容忽视的重要因素是访客可以获得新鲜的参观体验，以便他们能够快速放松和充电。

鉴于以上分析，我们可以得出结论，在当今时代看到这种现象并不奇怪。因此，可以预测中国博物馆数量及其访客数量在未来仍将保持这种增长趋势。
